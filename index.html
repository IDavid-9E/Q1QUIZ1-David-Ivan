Godzilla x Kong: The New Empire

An amazing monster movie about Godzilla and Kong joining forces together to defeat the Skar King three years after they defeated Mechagodzilla in the previous movie. It's also about Kong seeing more of his species in Hollow Earth to reunite with Godzilla in order to stop the Skar King from invading the Earth's surface.
